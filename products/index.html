SAFE ROOMS

Our safe rooms offer security and peace of mind. Blast resistant door systems, impervious panels, and localized ventilation offer protection from any hostile intrusion. Our saferooms provide complete protection in a discreet application.

Retrofitting any existing space is both functional and cost effective. A closet, an office, a wine cellar, virtually any room can double as a safe room.

Be it home invasion, nuclear, biological or chemical threat, we offer a full line of protection services. When you and what you value most enter the safe room, you will have security and peace of mind knowing the room is both discreet and impenetrable.

BLAST SHELTERS

Our blast shelters are custom designed structures able to withstand any catastrophic scenario. Whether it be home invasion, social chaos, nuclear, biological or chemical attack–our shelters offer security and autonomy for you and your loved ones.

Shelters are fitted with air filtration units, blast protection, and other various survival features. We do not install prefabricated steel structures. We design and build customized impervious underground structures with all the comforts of your existing home.

Accessing the blast shelter is designed to be simple and its intended use limited only by the imagination. Theaters, work out facilities, game rooms and wine cellars to name a few. During times of distress, you’ll have not only the security you desire, but the comfort of being at peace in a cozy home environment.

IMPERVIOUS STRUCTURES

Impervious Structures offer bullet and blast security protection in a discreet application virtually undetectable upon completion.

Standard window glazing is replaced with bullet and blast resistant window panels for maximum security. Walls, floors, and ceilings are fitted with customized panels to withstand bullet and blast scenarios of any type. Our door systems offer safety and security with state of the art locking mechanisms that are both bullet and blast resistant.

Impervious structures not only provide blast and ballistic protection, they are impervious to any would-be intrusion. When completed, the structure offers security and peace of mind knowing you and those you value most are in a secure environment.
